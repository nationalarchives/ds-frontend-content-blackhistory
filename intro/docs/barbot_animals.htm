African Wildlife
Here, a variety of animals are depicted against an imaginary African scene of hills, ships and a village. Some are named, others are easily identifiable. They include leopard, civet, rabbit, wild boar, deer and porcupine, and an elephant with strange ears.

This depiction of African wildlife is taken from the journals of Jean Barbot, who traded along the coast of West Africa in the 1670s and 1680s.

ADM 7/830B, f. 37 (1688)
