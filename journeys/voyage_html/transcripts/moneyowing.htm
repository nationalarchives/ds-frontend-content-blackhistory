Particulars in respect of future accounts.
TNA PRO ref: C 104/36 Pt 1 (1 April 1713)
Document Close window


Severall Perticulars to be brought to the Credit of the Generall Acco[un]t which
Cannot yet be Adjusted Viz The Produce of Goods sold the 17 Feb[rua]ry last for which time is given for paym[en]t to the 17th of this Instant Severall Perticulars remaining of former Sales which were not taken away nor Paid for by the Purchasers
Severall Goods Shiped off to save the Benifitt of the Draw back. Money oweing at Customhouse for Debentures of Goods Exported Money oweing for several Negroes
Sold in England Severall Goods remaining in the wareho[use] Unsold to be putt upto Sale Money to be Charged to the Officers and men for what they took to themselv[e]s
as Plunder w[hi]ch by their Agreem[en]t they were not to do. All which and what ever else may appear unaccounted for shall be adjusted as soon as possible.
