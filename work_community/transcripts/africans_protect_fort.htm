     I do Certify upon my word and honor, as an Officer and a gentleman, That on the Twenty fourth day of July, the Twenty fourth day of August and the Twenty fourth day of September 1809. I Mustered a Detachment of the Royal African Corps stationed at Gorée, at which respective periods I saw such Commissioned Officers, non Commissioned Officers and Private Men as are borne on the foregoing Muster Roll, & Quarterly Pay List, those for whom the true reasons assigned against their respective Names only excepted.
     I do likewise Certify, That all the sums sett down in the saide Muster Roll & Quarterly Pay List, amounting together to the sum of Four hundred & forty two pounds, fifteen shillings sterling, have been paid by me to the respective persons therein named, in strict conformance to the King's Regulations.
Dated at Goree this 30th September 1809
[signature] Edw[ar]d Lloyd Capt[ain]
