Dean Mahomed's Baths
In keeping with the fashion of the day Dean Mahomed opened his baths, in Brighton. The seaside town had become very important - sea bathing was now recommended for healthy living, and people were beginning to be concerned about bodily cleanliness.

British Library T1246, Opp. p.19 (c.1826)
