Précis of Intelligence

Dumaresq.
12 Nov[embe]r 1787
East Indies.

 Permit me to put You on your guard with regard to the East Indies - the French are determined to send as many Troops as possible - 4000 men are already ordered; the Reg[imen]t. de l'Isle de France of 1600 men arrived while I was there to embark for the Island of Bourbon - You may rest assured that they are very jealous of Great Britain in that quarter of the Globe
