To the Honourable the Court of DIRECTORS of the UNITED-COMPANY of Merchants of ENGLAND trading to the EAST-INDIES this View of BOMBAY done after the Painting in the Court Room of the Companys house in Leaden Hall Street is most humbly Dedicated by their HONOURS most obliged and most devoted Servant
John Bowles.
[crest] THE UNITED COMPANY OF MERCHANTS OF ENGLAND TRADING TO THE EAST INDIES
Printed for T. Bowles in St Pauls Church Yard & J. Bowles & Son, at the Black Horse in Cornhill.
E. Kirkall fecit
