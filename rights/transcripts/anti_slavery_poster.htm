NEGRO
APPRENTICES.
At a PUBLIC MEETING
OF THE BIRMINGHAM
ANTI-SLAVERY
SOCIETY,
Held the 12th. of September, 1836.
It was unanimously Resolved,
THAT a Copy of the Reports of the Corporal Punishments inflicted on the Apprenticed Negroes of Jamaica, by the Special Justices of that Colony, in the months of April, May, and June last, furnished by THEMSELVES, be published and placarded on the walls of Birmingham.
FLOGGING
BY ORDER OF
Special Magistrates.
